What this page is about

Every player wants the highlight, but most games are won by habits that look simple from the outside. I like the part of basketball where preparation shows up in footwork, timing, spacing, and knowing exactly where your next pass should go.

Game plan checklist
  1. Build trust before the ball finds you.
  2. Own the details that travel from game to game.
    • Talk early on defense.
    • Hit cuts on time.
    • Stay balanced on the catch.
  3. Finish strong even if the first half was ugly.

That same idea carries over into web design. Clean layouts, consistent spacing, readable typography, and working links are not flashy, but they make the entire experience better.
