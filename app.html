Shot Timing Game

Hot Hand Challenge

The idea is simple: press Shoot when the basketball lands in the green window. You get ten shots to build the best percentage you can. It is small on purpose, but still feels polished, responsive, and tied to the same basketball identity as the rest of the site.

Play

Time the release

The ball moves back and forth automatically. Makes happen in the center green band, close calls are considered rushed or late, and the game ends after ten attempts.

How it works

Clean, quick, competitive

This app keeps everything on one page, including styles and JavaScript, which fits the assignment requirement for the AI-assisted web app.

  • The green center is the perfect release point.
  • Yellow is close, but still a miss.
  • You get ten shots per round.
  • The ball speeds up slightly after every attempt.
  • Reset starts a fresh run immediately.

Best way to play: find a rhythm, trust the timing, and do not panic when the meter speeds up.
